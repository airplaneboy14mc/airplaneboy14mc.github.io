There are many versions of Minecraft that are a bit more obscure... They are a lot less well known, because they were discontinued early in their development, or after a certain console was discontinued from manufacturing, or even a better more platform friendly version was created, in which the device supported.

Raspberry Pi Edition:

Raspberry Pi edition was created for users on a Raspberry Pi, it played up to 1.12.2 I think. The Raspberry Pi Edition was soon discontinued.
